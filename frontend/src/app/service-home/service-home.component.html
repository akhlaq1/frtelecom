What exactly is a List Broker?

List brokers are individuals who can purchase lists containing prospective client information to use for direct marketing campaigns (mail, email or telemarketing usually) based on specified demographic guidelines. Lists purchased by list brokers can be as simple as name & address, or as detailed as name, address, job title, income, gender, and more. An experienced, professional broker will work with you no matter what the budget size, and should provide recommendations based on their knowledge of direct marketing. Be cautious of brokers who may try to sell you inaccurate and flawed data. If you are uncertain of the credibility of the list, just ask!
